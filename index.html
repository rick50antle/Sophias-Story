The Story

Read the introduction →

Sofia is an engineer who sketches a biodegradable water filter during a Rocky Mountain hike. She pursues it through two entities: ClearCurrent, LLC, which sells filters for profit, and The PureAccess Project, which distributes them to communities without clean water.

Her journey creates every accounting problem you need to understand — and you solve each one before learning what the formal system calls it.

You experience the concept first. Then you learn the name. That order matters.

How These Chapters Work

Experience First

You solve the problem before learning the term. Every concept is felt before it's named.

One Story, Start to Finish

Sofia's journey from a hiking sketch to two working entities creates every accounting problem organically.

Interactive Questions

35 questions with progressive hints. You sort, calculate, reflect, and build — not memorize.

Records Are a Lens

Accounting represents reality; it doesn't mirror it. You'll learn to see what the numbers show and what they miss.
